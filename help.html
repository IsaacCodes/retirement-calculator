Help

All of the terms listed on the calculator are defined here. All values already listed on the Calculator are defualts.


  • Current Savings: Amount of money you currently have saved for retirement.

  • Annual Contribution: Amount of money you add to the savings account each year.

  • Annual Return on Investment: Percent increase in the value of savings due to investments (ex. stocks). Added after Annual Contribution.

  • Retirement Age: Age you plan to retire. Retirement benefits start at 62 but you only get full benefits in California past age 66.

  • Estimated Lifespan: The estimated age you will live to. This is about 77 in the U.S. and 82 in California. However, it is best to save for a higher estimated lifespan than it is to run out of money early.

  • Annual Return in Retirement: Same as the Annual Return on Investment for pre-retirement, except instead it is for post-retirement.

  • Monthly Expenses: Estimated amount of money you will use per month in retirement.
